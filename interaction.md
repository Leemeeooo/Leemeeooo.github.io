# Portfolio Interaction Design

## Interactive Components

### 1. Article Category Filter & Search
- **Location**: Portfolio page
- **Functionality**: 
  - Left sidebar with category filters (Technology, Health, Crime, Entertainment, Culture, Business)
  - Search bar with real-time filtering
  - Grid layout displaying article cards with hover effects
  - Each card shows article title, category, date, and excerpt
  - Click to view full article details in modal or separate view

### 2. Writing Skills Radar Chart
- **Location**: About page
- **Functionality**:
  - Interactive radar chart showing expertise levels
  - Categories: SEO Writing, Investigative Reporting, Content Strategy, Social Media, Ghostwriting, Feature Writing
  - Hover effects reveal specific skill descriptions
  - Animated loading on page scroll

### 3. Contact Form with Validation
- **Location**: Contact page
- **Functionality**:
  - Multi-step contact form with smooth transitions
  - Fields: Name, Email, Project Type, Message, Budget Range
  - Real-time validation with visual feedback
  - Success animation on form submission
  - Integration with email service simulation

### 4. Article Reading Time Calculator
- **Location**: Individual article views
- **Functionality**:
  - Dynamic reading time estimation based on word count
  - Progress bar showing reading progress
  - Estimated completion time
  - Social sharing buttons with hover effects

## User Experience Flow

1. **Landing Experience**: Hero section with typewriter effect introducing Elizabeth's expertise
2. **Discovery**: Interactive portfolio grid with filtering capabilities
3. **Engagement**: Smooth scroll animations revealing content sections
4. **Connection**: Multi-step contact form with personalized experience
5. **Exploration**: Cross-page navigation with consistent visual effects

## Technical Implementation

- **Libraries**: Anime.js for animations, ECharts.js for radar chart, Typed.js for typewriter effect
- **Effects**: Scroll-triggered animations, hover transformations, smooth page transitions
- **Responsive**: Mobile-first design with touch-friendly interactions
